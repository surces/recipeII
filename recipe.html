Nigerian Indomie Noodles

Author : Success Banwuzia

Nigerian-style Indomie is a delicious twist on instant noodles, made by stir-frying with vegetables, peppers, and eggs to create a fast, spicy, and satisfying meal.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Indomie noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 fresh tomato, chopped
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • Seasoning (from the noodle pack)

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles in water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, tomatoes, and pepper for 2 minutes.
  3. Crack eggs into the pan and scramble with the vegetables until cooked.
  4. Add the boiled noodles and seasoning from the pack.
  5. Stir-fry everything together for 2-3 minutes. Serve hot!

Tips

  • Use more vegetables like bell peppers or carrots for extra nutrition.
  • For extra heat, add more scotch bonnet or chili flakes.
